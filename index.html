About Me

I was born in Chicago and raised in its northern suburbs with my twin brother and sister. I received my BS from Bradley University in Communications with a sports concentration. After graduating from BU, I enhanced my computer skills by completing coursework in After Effects, Premiere Pro, and Avid (Media Composer). Following that, I spent the next fifteen months interning at Big Shoulders Digital Video Productions in Chicago where I edited videos and assisted with studio and field shoots. Now I am enrolled in Northwestern University's Coding Program where I hope to learn the necessary skills to gain employment in the field. When I have down time, I love to ski (preferably in the west) and vacation in Martha's Vineyard. My constant companion when I am doing any of this is my dog, Scout.
